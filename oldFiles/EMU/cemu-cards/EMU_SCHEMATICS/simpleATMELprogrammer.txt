simple programmer

o Flash the atmel at90s2313. Need 3 resistors 220ohm, 
1 - 20 pin ic socket and 
DB25male to plug into the printer port. 
All 3 of the resistors are tied to pin 20 (Vcc) or 
2313 pin 19,20,21,22,23,24,25 
all going to pin 10 (GND) of the 2313. 

DB25M-res----2313 
2---220ohm---20 
3---220ohm---20 
4---220ohm---20 
5------------5 
6------------1 
7------------17 
8------------19 
10-----------18 
19to25-------10
